## Full Description (for itch.io page)

### 🥊 Punch Through Politics!

Ever wanted to see a generalissimo face off against a demagogue in hand-to-hand combat? Now you can!

**Political Fighting Game** is a fast-paced, satirical 2D brawler featuring controversial politicians and historical figures as playable characters. Built for laughs, local multiplayer chaos, and sharp political commentary.

### ✨ Features

- **2 Playable Characters** (MVP): The Generalissimo vs The Demagogue
- **Local 2-Player Versus Mode**: Grab a friend and battle on the same device
- **Unique Movesets**: Each character has signature attacks reflecting their persona
- **Combo System**: Chain attacks for devastating damage
- **Blocking Mechanic**: Strategic defense with damage/knockback reduction
- **Sound & Juice**: Screen shake, hit particles, audio feedback

### 🎮 How to Play

**Player 1 Controls**:
- WASD: Move/Jump
- J: Light attack
- K: Heavy attack
- L: Special attack
- I: Block

**Player 2 Controls**:
- Arrow Keys: Move/Jump
- Numpad 1: Light attack
- Numpad 2: Heavy attack
- Numpad 3: Special attack
- Numpad 0: Block

**Win Conditions**:
- First to 2 round wins takes the match
- Knock out your opponent's health or outlast the 99-second timer

### 🛠️ Tech Stack

Built with **Godot 4.x** (open-source game engine). Runs in your browser via HTML5/WebGL - no downloads, no installs!
